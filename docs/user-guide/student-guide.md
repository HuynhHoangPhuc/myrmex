# Student Guide

## Registration

Students are registered via invite code issued by the admin.

### Option A: Invite Code + Email
1. Receive your invite code and temporary password from the admin.
2. Go to the login page → click **Register with Invite Code**.
3. Enter invite code, your institutional email, and temporary password → **Activate Account**.
4. Set a new permanent password → **Save**.
5. You are redirected to the Dashboard.

### Option B: Microsoft OAuth (SSO)
1. Go to the login page → click **Continue with Microsoft**.
2. Sign in with your institutional Microsoft account.
3. If your email matches a pre-registered invite, your account is activated automatically.
4. You are redirected to the Dashboard.

> If Microsoft login fails with "No invite found", contact your admin to ensure your email is pre-registered.

## Viewing Your Schedule

1. Navigate to **Timetable → Schedules** (`/timetable/schedules`).
2. Your enrolled sessions are shown for the current semester by default.
3. Use the **Semester** dropdown to view past or upcoming semesters.
4. Each row shows: subject name, teacher, room, day, time slot.
5. Click a row for full session details.

---

## Viewing Enrolled Subjects

1. Navigate to **Subjects → Offerings** (`/subjects/offerings`).
2. Filter by current semester to see subjects you are enrolled in.
3. Each subject shows: code, name, credits, teacher, enrollment status.

---

## Requesting Subject Enrollment

1. Navigate to **Subjects → Offerings** (`/subjects/offerings`).
2. Browse available subjects for the current semester.
3. Check the **Prerequisites** column — you must have passed all prerequisites to enroll.
4. Click **Request Enrollment** on the desired subject.
5. If prerequisites are met, the request is submitted to the admin for approval.
6. You will receive an in-app notification when your enrollment is approved or rejected.

### Enrollment Status Values

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | Request submitted, awaiting admin approval |
| Enrolled | Approved and confirmed |
| Rejected | Denied — check notification for reason |
| Waitlisted | Subject full; you are in queue |

---

## Checking Prerequisite Status

1. Navigate to **Subjects → Prerequisites** (`/subjects/prerequisites`).
2. Select a subject to see its prerequisite chain (displayed as a dependency graph).
3. Your completed subjects are highlighted in green; missing ones in red.
4. A subject is enrollable when all prerequisites show green.

---

## Viewing Transcript & GPA

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** — your current GPA is shown in the summary card.
2. For the full transcript, navigate to **Students** → your profile → **Transcript** tab.
3. The transcript lists all completed semesters, subjects, credits, and grades.
4. Use the **Export PDF** button to download your transcript.

> Grades are only visible after the teacher publishes them. Contact your teacher if a grade is missing after the grading deadline.
